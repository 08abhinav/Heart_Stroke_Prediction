How Our Model Works

CardioPredict uses a machine learning model trained on real-world health data to assess your risk for heart-related issues. It considers medical factors like your age, BMI, glucose level, and lifestyle choices like smoking or physical activity. After you provide these inputs, our model instantly calculates your likelihood of having a potential heart stroke.

What We Need From You

  • Age
  • Hypertension (Yes/No)
  • Heart Disease History (Yes/No)
  • Average Glucose Level
  • BMI (Body Mass Index)
  • Smoking Status

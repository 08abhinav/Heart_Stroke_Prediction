About CardioPredict

CardioPredict is an AI-powered web application designed to help users assess their risk of stroke based on health data inputs. Our goal is to enable early detection and promote awareness by analyzing personal health metrics using machine learning models.

Why CardioPredict?

  • Early stroke risk assessment based on medical features
  • User-friendly interface with quick predictions
  • Built using Python, Flask, and machine learning
  • Powered by TailwindCSS for a sleek design

Our Mission

We aim to build accessible and data-driven tools that empower individuals to take control of their heart health and make informed lifestyle choices.
